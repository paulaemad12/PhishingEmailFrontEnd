Website Overview

Welcome to the Phishing Simulation and Training Platform! This platform is designed to help users understand and mitigate phishing threats through interactive simulations, password strength checking, and engaging cybersecurity games.

Features:

  • Phishing Simulation: Analyze email content to detect potential phishing attempts.
  • Password Strength Checker: Evaluate and improve your password security.
  • Cybersecurity Games: Learn through interactive quizzes and challenges.

Our goal is to enhance your cybersecurity awareness and empower you with the tools to protect yourself and your organization from evolving cyber threats.
